## 🤔 Decisions

Five design calls locked during the walk. All five are recorded in `_Decisions.md` with full reasoning:

- **Title-on-nametag stays.** Sanity check after PR #9's level-row strip — the strip's reasoning ("level is also in XPBar, doubled surface is loud") doesn't transfer to titles since titles are nowhere else and they're identity-side, not progression-side.
- **`fell_asleep_here` AFK threshold drops 30 → 19 minutes.** Roblox's auto-disconnect kicks in at 20 min, so 30 was unreachable. 19 lands just inside the disconnect window. Side-note carried to AchievementTracker: focus-based `AfkDetector` may not be the right signal — idle-based detection (`Player.Idled`) probably is for "fell asleep at the keyboard" semantics.
- **Combined fade `level N — new title: X` at 5s hold.** When a milestone level-up coincides with a title unlock, merge into one fade. Bump unlock notification hold from 2s → 5s (hover-show on the XPBar stays at 2s — different lane, different intent).
- **TitleMenu build approach: ship a placeholder, defer the polished menu.** Functional placeholder lands in MVP-2; polished v2 menu is its own brief with a focused Tyler-led design session, drops in over the placeholder. Placeholder gets explicit `_Cleanup_Backlog.md` entry to prevent quiet permanence.
- **MVP brief split into MVP-1 / MVP-2.** MVP-1 = data + display (this session, shipped as PR #12). MVP-2 = placeholder menu + manual equip + production-cutover migration. Reason: smaller blast radius per brief, easier playtest scope, half-feature mid-state ("auto-equip-highest only") is still a complete visible feature.
