# Tone & Rules

The single source of truth for what Sad Cave **is** and **is not**.
Read this before designing any new feature.

---

## 🌑 What Sad Cave Is

- A **quiet emotional exploration / hangout game**
- **60% exploration / progression**, **40% social hangout**
- A place players enter to **relax, reflect, talk, and discover**
- Moody, calm, atmospheric, premium
- Progression that feels **meaningful, not grindy**
- Subtle, human, intentional

---

## 🚫 What Sad Cave Is NOT

- Not a simulator
- Not loud, flashy, or chaotic
- Not a grind game
- Not a pet/currency/leaderboard race
- Not aggressive monetization
- Not cringe or try-hard in tone

---

## 🎨 Design Rules

1. **Mood first.** If a feature would break the calm, it doesn't ship — even if it would boost retention.
2. **Simple and scalable.** Beginner-friendly systems. No overengineering.
3. **Premium UI.** Dark, clean, subtle. No bright colors, no clutter, no emoji spam in-game.
4. **Dialogue is short, human, emotional, not cringe.** Read it out loud — if it sounds fake, rewrite it.
5. **Progression rewards presence**, not farming. Time spent existing in the space matters more than tasks completed.
6. **Every system must support the atmosphere.** If a system doesn't reinforce mood, presence, or quiet discovery, it doesn't belong.
7. **No excessive currencies.** Maybe one soft currency or none. Never multiple.
8. **No pets.** No flashy rewards. No "+999 XP!" popups.
9. **Monetization stays gentle** — cosmetic, optional, never gated emotion or core experience.

---

## 🗣 Dialogue Voice

- Short lines. Often under 10 words.
- Emotional but not melodramatic.
- Leaves space — silence is a tool.
- The player is treated like a person, not a quest-giver target.
- NPCs feel like they have inner lives, not scripts.

**Good:**
> "You came back."
> "It's quieter when you're here."

**Bad:**
> "Welcome adventurer! Complete this quest for +50 gold!"
> "Hey bestie, soooo glad you're here!! 💖"

---

## 🎛 UI Principles

- Dark backgrounds. Soft, low-saturation accents.
- Generous spacing. Nothing crammed.
- Type: clean, readable, slightly understated.
- Animations: slow fades, gentle eases. No bounce, no pop.
- Sound: minimal UI sounds — soft clicks at most, often nothing.

---

## ✅ Feature Sanity Check

Before building anything, ask:
- Does it support mood, presence, or discovery?
- Would a calm player enjoy this, or would it stress them out?
- Could a simulator have this? If yes — be very careful.
- Is the simplest version of this idea enough?

If two or more answers are "no" or "not sure" — park it in [[08_Ideas_Parking_Lot/_Parking_Lot]] and don't build it yet.
