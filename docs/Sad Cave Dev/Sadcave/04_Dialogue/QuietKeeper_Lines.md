# QuietKeeper — Lines

Working draft of QuietKeeper's dialogue. Keep it short. Read it out loud.

See character: [[../05_NPCs/QuietKeeper]]
Voice rules: [[../01_Vision/Tone_and_Rules]]

---

## First Meeting

**QK:** "Oh. Hello."
**QK:** "You found your way in."
**QK:** "Stay as long as you'd like."

**Choices:**
- "Who are you?"
- "Where am I?"
- _(walk away — no choice taken)_

---

## Returning Player

**QK:** "You came back."
**QK:** "It's quieter when you're here."

---

## Late / Long Session

**QK:** "Still here?"
**QK:** "That's alright. So am I."

---

## Scratch
- "I don't really keep track of time anymore."
- "Some places ask for things. This one doesn't."
- "Sit if you want. The stone's warm enough."

---

## Notes
- QK never gives quests. Never explains rules. Never pushes.
- Speaks like someone who's been alone a long time and is gently surprised by company.
